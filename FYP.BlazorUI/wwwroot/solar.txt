Solar energy is power derived from sunlight using solar panels.
It is renewable, abundant, and sustainable.
Common solar myths:
1. Solar panels don't work on cloudy days – False, they still generate power.
2. Solar is too expensive – False, costs have dropped significantly.
3. Solar panels require constant maintenance – False, minimal upkeep is needed.
Benefits of solar:
- Reduces electricity bills
- Eco-friendly and reduces carbon footprint
- Increases property value
